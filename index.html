Our Story

Breaking Down Barriers,
One Game at a Time

Sports Beyond Borders was founded on a simple belief: the lack of equipment should never be the reason a child misses out on sport. We collect, refurbish, and distribute sporting gear to communities that need it most — because access to play is a right, not a privilege.

Through our programs, we don't just hand over equipment. We partner with local coaches, schools, and community centers to build lasting sports cultures that foster teamwork, resilience, and belonging.

What We Do

Our Programs

Three pillars drive everything we do — collecting gear, building skills, and growing community.

🎒

Gear Drive

We run year-round equipment drives, collecting gently used sporting goods from donors across the country. Every item is inspected, cleaned, and matched to a community in need.

  • Soccer balls, cleats & jerseys
  • Basketball hoops & shoes
  • Baseball gloves & helmets
  • And 15 more sports
Core Program
🏟️

Community Courts

We partner with local organizations to establish dedicated play spaces in underserved neighborhoods — from painted asphalt courts to full multi-sport fields.

  • Infrastructure support
  • Ongoing equipment supply
  • Certified coach placement
  • Youth league coordination
🎓

Athlete Pathways

For young athletes showing exceptional potential, we provide mentorship, scholarship support, and pathways to regional and national competitions.

  • 1-on-1 mentorship
  • Academic scholarships
  • Competition sponsorships
  • Alumni network access
